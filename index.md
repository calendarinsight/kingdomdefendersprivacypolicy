# Privacy Policy for Kingdom Defenders Riftfall

**Last Updated: January 13, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Kingdom Defenders Riftfall ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information collected during your use of our mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and disclosure.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Player Name**: An optional display name for leaderboards (default is auto-generated like "Player_A7B3")

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Identifier**: A randomly generated anonymous ID stored locally on your device to save your game progress
- **Game Progress**: Your level completion, scores, and achievement data
- **Device Performance Data**: Basic device capability information used solely to optimize game performance

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do NOT collect:
- ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Contacts or address book
- ❌ Photos, videos, or files
- ❌ Financial or payment information
- ❌ Advertising identifiers
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Microphone or camera data

## How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used to:
- Save your game progress between sessions
- Display your scores on optional leaderboards
- Track your in-game achievements
- Optimize game performance for your device

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Most game data is stored locally on your device
- **Cloud Storage**: Leaderboard scores are stored securely in our cloud database
- **Security**: We use industry-standard security measures to protect stored data
- **No Selling**: We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties

## Data Retention

- Local game data remains on your device until you uninstall the game or clear app data
- Leaderboard data is retained to maintain high score records
- You may request deletion of your leaderboard data by contacting us

## Children's Privacy

Kingdom Defenders Riftfall is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The game can be played without providing any personal information.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Play the game without creating an account or providing personal information
- Use a random auto-generated player name instead of a custom one
- Request deletion of your leaderboard data
- Uninstall the game to remove all local data

## Third-Party Services

- **Cloud Database**: For storing leaderboard scores (anonymous data only)

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.
